About enhio

enhio.com is a free text analysis tool that runs entirely in your browser. Paste any text and instantly see word count, readability scores, word frequency analysis, sentence structure, tone detection, and actionable writing suggestions.

No data is sent to any server. Your text stays in your browser. The analysis runs in real time as you type.

What It Analyzes

  • Word, character, sentence, and paragraph counts
  • Reading time estimate (200 words per minute)
  • Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level (how many years of education needed)
  • Flesch Reading Ease (0-100 scale, higher is easier)
  • Top 20 most frequent words (excluding stop words)
  • Sentence length distribution (short, medium, long)
  • Tone detection (formal vs casual based on contractions, vocabulary complexity, sentence length)
  • Writing suggestions: flags long sentences, passive voice, overused words
