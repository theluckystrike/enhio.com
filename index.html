How the Text Enhancement Tool Works

Enhio is a comprehensive text analysis tool that evaluates your writing across multiple dimensions simultaneously. When you paste text into the editor, the tool instantly computes word count, character count, sentence count, paragraph count, syllable count, and estimated reading time. These basic metrics provide a quick snapshot of your content's structure before diving into deeper analysis.

The readability engine calculates both Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level and Flesch Reading Ease scores using standard linguistic formulas. Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level tells you the U.S. school grade required to understand the text, while Flesch Reading Ease produces a 0-100 score where higher numbers mean easier reading. The tool uses vowel-group syllable counting with silent-e adjustment for accurate English syllable estimation. These scores help writers match their content's complexity to their target audience — whether that is a grade-school reader or a graduate-level researcher.

Beyond readability, Enhio performs word frequency analysis that excludes common stop words (the, and, is, etc.) to surface your most-used content words. This reveals unconscious word preferences and helps identify keyword density for SEO purposes. The sentence length distribution chart shows you the mix of short, medium, and long sentences — a key predictor of reader engagement. Research shows the optimal distribution is roughly 30% short sentences, 50% medium, and 20% long.

Features

The Analyze mode provides real-time readability scoring, word frequency tables, sentence length distribution, tone analysis that detects formal vs. informal register, and actionable writing suggestions including passive voice alerts and overlong sentence warnings. The SEO Analysis mode lets you enter a target keyword and evaluates keyword density, placement in the first 100 words, heading usage, and generates an SEO readiness score from 0 to 100. The Side-by-Side Compare mode allows you to paste two versions of text and instantly compare their readability metrics, helping you choose the stronger draft.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to check readability of text?

Paste your text into enhio.com for instant readability scores including Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level and Flesch Reading Ease. The tool also analyzes sentence length distribution, word frequency, tone, and provides actionable improvement suggestions — all processed client-side in your browser.

What is Flesch-Kincaid score?

Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level indicates the U.S. school grade needed to understand the text. A score of 8 means 8th-grade reading level. Flesch Reading Ease ranges from 0-100, where 60-70 is plain English understood by 13-15 year olds. Both use sentence length and syllable counts in their formulas.

What is the best readability checker?

enhio.com provides comprehensive text analysis beyond just readability: word frequency, tone detection, passive voice alerts, SEO keyword analysis, and side-by-side content comparison. It processes everything client-side (no data sent to servers) and requires no sign-up.

What is a good readability score for blog posts?

For blog posts, aim for a Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level of 6-8 (middle school reading level) and a Flesch Reading Ease of 60-70. This makes content accessible to the widest audience. Academic writing may target grade 12+, while marketing copy should stay below grade 6.

How to optimize content for SEO readability?

Use short sentences (under 20 words), short paragraphs (2-3 sentences), and simple words. Include your target keyword in the first 100 words with 1-3% density. Use our SEO Analysis tab to check keyword placement, density, and get an SEO readability score from 0-100.

Is this text analyzer free?

Yes, enhio.com is completely free with no sign-up required. All analysis is performed instantly in your browser — your text is never sent to any server, ensuring complete privacy.

Can I compare two versions of text side by side?

Yes, enhio.com has a Side-by-Side Compare mode where you can paste Version A and Version B to see readability and metrics differences instantly. This is useful for comparing headline variations, testing different paragraph structures, or evaluating rewrites.

Does enhio.com support SEO keyword analysis?

Yes, the SEO Analysis tab lets you enter a target keyword and evaluates its density, placement in the first 100 words, heading inclusion, and generates an overall SEO readiness score from 0 to 100.
